# Flipkart Mobile Sales EDA – Final Report

## 1. Dataset Overview
The dataset contains information about mobile phones sold on Flipkart, including:
- Brand
- Selling Price
- Rating
- Discount
- Color
- RAM
- Storage

After cleaning, the dataset was ready for analysis with no major missing values
in key columns.

---

## 2. Univariate Analysis

**Brand Distribution**
- Samsung, Apple, and Oppo have the most products listed.

**Price Distribution**
- Most phones are priced between ₹10,000–₹20,000.

**Ratings**
- Majority of phones have ratings ≥ 4, indicating high customer satisfaction.

**Color Preference**
- Black is the most preferred phone color.

**Visualizations Used**
- Bar plots
- Histograms
- Countplots

---

## 3. Bivariate Analysis

**Price vs Brand**
- Premium brands like Apple and Samsung have higher average prices.

**Rating vs Brand**
- High-rated phones are mostly from Apple, Samsung, and a few niche brands.

**Price vs Discount**
- Many mid-range phones have moderate discounts.
- Pairplot shows relationships between Selling Price, Discount, and Rating.

**Visualizations Used**
- Boxplots
- Scatterplots
- Pairplots

---

## 4. Multivariate / Advanced Analysis

- **Pairplot**: Explored relationships between Selling Price, Discount, and Rating
  to identify trends and potential outliers.
- **Correlation Analysis**: Positive correlation observed between price and rating
  for premium brands.

---

## 6. Conclusion

- **Consumer Trends**: Mid-range smartphones dominate, with preference for
  reliable brands and black color.
- **Brand Strategy Insights**: Premium brands like Apple and Samsung maintain
  both popularity and high pricing.
- **EDA Summary**: This analysis provides clear insights into pricing trends,
  brand popularity, customer ratings, and color preference, which can guide
  marketing or inventory decisions on Flipkart.
